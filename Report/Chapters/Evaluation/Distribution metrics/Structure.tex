\section{Distribution Distances}
\label{evaluation:distribution}



To illustrate the comparison methods described in this section, two beta distributions are used for example calculations, both distributions are scaled and translated to the interval $[-3;3]$

\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    F(x) = Beta(2,2; -3,3) \\
    G(g) = Beta(7,11,-3;3)
\end{split}
\end{equation}

The two distributions are visualised in \cref{fig:data:exampledist}.
